No. We talk to founders and operators, not engineers. Everything we deliver is explained in plain language and tied to the business questions that matter — the technical work stays documented for whoever needs it.

That's the typical case. Most clients arrive with GA4, Meta Pixel, and a CRM already wired up — and a sneaking suspicion none of them agree. Week 1 is a Foundation Audit: we map what's there, flag what's broken, and give you a prioritized fix list before changing anything. Existing setup is the starting point, not a problem.
